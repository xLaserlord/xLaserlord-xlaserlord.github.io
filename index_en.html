Projekt

The goal

Topics like Internet of Things (IoT) and automation are getting more and more interesting and gave us the idea for our diploma thesis. Our goal is to provide a smart greenhouse.

The plan

Sensors measure various parameters such as temperature, soil moisture and wind speed, which are then processed on a mini computer. This data is displayed on a web app.
